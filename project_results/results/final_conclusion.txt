FINAL CONCLUSION - CAT BREED CLASSIFICATION PROJECT
================================================================================

Dataset: Kaggle 'nikolasgegenava/cat-breeds' (Top 10 classes by image count)
Total Images: ~5,000 images (~200 per class)
Classes: Abyssinian, Bombay, Egyptian Mau, Exotic Shorthair, Himalayan, Maine Coon, Russian Blue, Scottish Fold, Siamese, Sphynx

MODELS TRAINED:
- ResNet50
- DenseNet121  
- MobileNetV3Small

RESULTS SUMMARY:

For the cat breed classification task using the Kaggle dataset with top 10 classes,
the model with the highest test accuracy was ResNet50 
with an accuracy of 0.9560.

The model with the highest mean average precision was ResNet50 
with an mAP of 0.9902.

The fastest model to train was MobileNetV3Small, 
with a training time of 9.54 minutes.

RECOMMENDATION:

The final best model should be chosen by considering accuracy, mAP, training time,
parameter count, and confusion matrix results together. If one model has slightly
lower accuracy but trains much faster and has fewer parameters, it may be more 
suitable for practical deployment.
